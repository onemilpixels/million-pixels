## Engagement Tactics (in order of leverage)

1. **Reply guy — but smart.** First-30-min replies on big indie/internet accounts → max impressions. Substantive, not "🔥🔥🔥".
2. **Quote-tweets with personality.** When MDHP comes up anywhere, QT with our take.
3. **Tag big accounts SPARINGLY.** Tag spam = ratio. One genuine tag/week max.
4. **Number reveals.** "We just crossed [milestone]" tweets get 5–10× normal engagement.
5. **Threads on Sundays.** Long-form, story-driven. Best growth day on X.
6. **Drama-adjacent.** When MDHP / pixel-art / indie discourse erupts, jump in with a take, not a CTA.

## Hashtag Strategy
Hashtags do almost nothing on X anymore — but a few work:
- #BuildInPublic (active community)
- #IndieHackers
- #SideProject (Saturday)
- Avoid: #marketing, #startup (bot zones)

Max 2 hashtags/tweet. Most posts: zero.

---

## Trend-Jacking Rules
Watch trends 2x/day. Only jump in when:
1. The trend genuinely overlaps (internet history, pixel art, indie business).
2. We can add value, not just CTA.
3. The angle is funny or original, not forced.

Never trend-jack tragedy / politics / news. Brand-safe always.

---

## Partner Co-Posting Playbook
When an influencer agrees:
1. We draft 2–3 tweet/post options for them, NO required script.
2. They pick / modify / write their own.
3. Their post goes live with their referral link.
4. We quote-tweet within 1 hour with thanks + amplify.
5. Stagger partners — max 1 partner post amplified per 24h.

## Risk: Twitter Algo / Account Health
- No follow-unfollow.
- No mass-reply spam (>30 replies in 10 min triggers spam detection).
- No buying followers.
- No suspicious-link cloaking — direct millionpixels.example only, no shorteners on launch.
- Disclosure: Partner posts must include #ad on FTC grounds; we will not retweet partner posts that don't disclose.
