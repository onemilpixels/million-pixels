## Decisions Travis Needs to Make Before Launch
1. **Cash bonus pool yes/no.** Strict "no cash" influencers, but organic ambassadors get $1k/wk pool? Or all-pixels-no-cash everywhere? See `REFERRAL_AMBASSADOR_PROGRAM.md`.
2. **Refund policy length.** 14 days recommended. Stripe default is 120 days for disputes either way.
3. **Pixel content moderation.** What's banned on the canvas? (NSFW, hate, competitor logos, broken links.) Need a published Acceptable Use Policy before partners go live or this becomes a fire.
4. **Founder face level.** Travis on camera in launch thread = 3–5x conversion. Anonymous = safer but slower trust build. Recommend on-camera.
5. **Influencer commission permanence.** "Lifetime 10%" vs. "12 months". Recommend 12 months — protects long-tail margin without scaring partners off.
6. **Discord moderation team.** Travis solo for Week 1, recruit 2 trusted mods by Day 14.
