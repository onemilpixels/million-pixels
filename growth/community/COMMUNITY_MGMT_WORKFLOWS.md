# Community Management Workflows

**Principle:** Every comment / DM / mention is a chance to convert OR to build a long-term advocate. Slow = death. Default response SLA: ≤30 minutes during 8am–11pm PT, ≤8 hours overnight.

---

## Inbox Sources (single dashboard, eventually)
1. Discord — DMs to founder, mentions in any channel, tickets
2. Twitter — replies, QTs, DMs
3. Instagram — DMs, comments on our posts, comments on partner posts (#ad)
4. TikTok — comments on our posts and partner videos with our code
5. Reddit — DMs, replies on our posts and any post mentioning Million Pixels
6. Email — hello@millionpixels.example
7. On-site support — `?support=1` ticket form

**Tool:** Until volume justifies a real tool (Front, Help Scout), use a shared Notion / Linear inbox with one row per ticket. Migrate to a real tool by Day 30.

## Response Tree

### Comment / DM type → action

| Type | SLA | Action |
|---|---|---|
| Buying question ("how do I buy?") | 15 min | Reply with link to buying guide + offer help in DMs |
| Tech issue (didn't get pixel, image not showing) | 30 min | Open ticket, attempt fix, follow up within 2h |
| Refund request | 1h | Per refund policy: full refund within 14 days, no questions, kind tone, ask what went wrong |
| Influencer / partner inquiry | 4h | Triage to partner pipeline, send T2 template |
| Press / podcast inquiry | 8h | Triage to founder; calendar link |
| Hate / troll | n/a | Ignore, mute. Don't engage. Block if persistent. |
| Genuine criticism | 4h | Thank, address publicly, share fix |
| Spam / scam | 5 min | Delete, ban, optionally screenshot for warning post |
| Compliment / love | 1h | Thank personally, ask for referral / share |
| User showing off their pixel | 30 min | Celebrate publicly, QT, add to spotlight queue |
| "Is this a scam?" | 30 min | Empathetic + proof: Stripe, refund policy, public metrics, founder face |

## Canned Responses (personalize each time)

**Buying help:**
> Hey! Buying guide: [link]. If anything in there is unclear, just reply and I'll walk you through it. Refunds work for 14 days no-questions if anything goes sideways. — Travis

**Tech issue:**
> Sorry that happened. Can you share: 1) order # from the receipt email, 2) screenshot of what you're seeing? I'll fix it within a few hours — and if I can't, full refund. — Travis

**Refund:**
> No problem at all — refund processed. It'll show on your card in 5–10 business days. If you'd be willing to share what went wrong (didn't like the design tool? confused on checkout? changed mind?) it'd help a ton. Either way, thanks for trying it. — Travis

**Scam concern:**
> Fair question. Some specifics: payments via Stripe (so chargeback works normally), 14-day refund policy at millionpixels.example/refunds, daily metrics public at [URL], my real-name handle is @travis_handle. Happy to answer anything more specific. — Travis

**Press inquiry:**
> Thanks for reaching out — happy to chat. Calendar link: [Calendly URL]. Daily metrics + backstory deck attached if useful. — Travis

**Generic thanks:**
> Thanks for buying a pixel 🟪 — drop a screenshot of it in #pixel-showcase on the Discord if you want a shoutout. Refer a friend with your code [CODE] and you'll start climbing the leaderboard.

## Escalation Triggers (→ Travis directly)
- Refunds >$100 in one ask
- Legal threat (any wording: "lawyer", "sue", "trademark", "DMCA")
- Press from a Tier-1 outlet (Verge, NYT, WIRED, BBC, TechCrunch)
- Influencer Tier S responding
- Public Twitter blowup (>50 hostile replies in <1h)
- Stripe / payment processor flag
- Suspicious activity (chargeback pattern, refund abuse, gaming the referral pool)

## Voice & Tone

- **First-person.** "I" not "we" until we have a team. Travis is the human.
- **Concise.** ≤3 sentences default.
- **Warm.** Names, emojis used sparingly (🟪 ✅ 🙏 — not 🔥💯🚀).
- **Direct on bad news.** No "we sincerely apologize for the inconvenience."
- **Cheeky-but-kind on dumb questions.** Never sarcastic when someone is confused or frustrated.
- **No marketingspeak.** No "leverage", "synergy", "ecosystem", "value-add".
