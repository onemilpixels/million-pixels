## Outreach Cadence
- **Week 1 (Days 1–7):** Hit Tier A (indie/build-in-public) — warmest audience, fastest yes. Goal: 15 signed.
- **Week 2 (Days 8–14):** Tier S internet-history creators — high-leverage, slow. Plus Tier B design. Goal: +20 signed (35 total).
- **Week 3 (Days 15–21):** Tier C finance/side-hustle + Tier E meme pages. Goal: +35 signed (70 total).
- **Week 4 (Days 22–30):** Tier D tech + follow-up wave + referrals from signed influencers. Goal: 100 signed.

## Selection Filter (Before Outreach)
1. ≥3% avg engagement rate (likes+comments / followers on last 10 posts)
2. Audience matches: 18–35, US/UK/CA/AU heavy, English-speaking
3. Not currently in a brand exclusivity deal (check last 5 posts for #ad)
4. Has done unpaid shoutouts before (genuine voice) OR has done affiliate deals
5. Comments section feels alive (not bot farm)

## Disclosure Note
US FTC requires #ad / #sponsored disclosure when commission is involved. All outreach scripts include this requirement. UK ASA + EU equivalents similar. Non-negotiable.
