# Influencer Outreach Templates

**Rules of the road:**
1. Personalize line 1 — reference a recent post by name. Generic = ignored.
2. Lead with the gift, not the ask.
3. ≤120 words. They read DMs on phones.
4. One CTA. One link. One tracking code.
5. Always include FTC disclosure requirement.
6. Follow up exactly once after 5 days. Then move on.

## REJECTION / NO-RESPONSE HANDLING
- **Soft no ("not right now"):** Reply "Totally — pixels stay reserved 30 days if anything changes. Best of luck with [their project]." Add to nurture list.
- **Hard no:** "All good, appreciate the reply." Remove from list, don't recontact for 90 days.
- **Ghost after follow-up:** Mark inactive, requeue at Day 60 with new metric proof.

## TRACKING CODE FORMAT
- Pattern: First 6 letters of handle, UPPERCASE, no spaces (e.g., `LEVELSIO`, `GREGIS`).
- Stored in OUTREACH_TRACKER.csv `referral_code` column.
- Each code maps to: 10% commission, lifetime, paid quarterly via Stripe Connect or PayPal.

## RECIPROCITY MOVES (Before First DM)
1. Genuinely engage with 3 of their recent posts (like + thoughtful comment) over 5–7 days before DMing.
2. Quote-tweet or share one of their pieces with sincere commentary.
3. THEN reach out. Cold DMs without warmth = ignored.
