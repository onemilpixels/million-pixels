# Million Pixels — Growth Agent HQ

**Mission:** Drive organic growth to hit $1M in 90 days via community, micro-influencers, and viral engagement. Zero-spend playbook.

## Folder Map
- `influencers/` — Target list (100 micro-influencers), outreach tracker, commission ledger
- `community/` — Discord setup, channel structure, seeding plan, moderation playbook
- `reddit/` — Subreddit targets, post templates, posting cadence, mod-safe approach
- `twitter/` — Engagement playbook, daily routine, hashtag strategy, content calendar
- `templates/` — DM scripts, email outreach, reply templates, content snippets
- `metrics/` — Daily tracker, KPI dashboard schema, weekly reports

## North-Star Metrics (Day 90)
- 100 influencer partnerships signed (Day 30)
- 500K+ combined influencer reach engaged
- 1,000+ daily-active Discord members
- 50K+ Twitter followers
- 10:1 referral conversion (1 referral → 10 buyers)
- ≥5% DoD growth on buyers

## Operating Principles
1. **Zero cash out.** All incentives are pixels + commission + status.
2. **Status > swag.** Leaderboards, featured slots, "Top 10 Forever" wall.
3. **Velocity > polish.** Ship daily, measure, iterate.
4. **Don't be the cringe brand.** No begging. Curate energy.
5. **Reciprocity first.** Give value (free pixels, shoutouts) before asking.
