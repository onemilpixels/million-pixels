# 🎬 AD CREATIVE ANGLES & SCRIPTS
**5 core angles × 3+ creatives each = 15+ ready-to-shoot ad concepts**

---

## CREATIVE PRINCIPLES

1. **First 3 seconds = everything** (TikTok/IG); first 1 second = title for Reddit/Google
2. **Native > polished** — phone-shot beats studio every time on social
3. **Pattern interrupt opening** — visual surprise, bold claim, or counterintuitive hook
4. **Show the product** — always include the pixel grid filling
5. **Single CTA** — "Get your pixel at [URL]"

---

## ANGLE 1: 🔥 FOMO / URGENCY ("Prices Rising")

### Concept 1A: "The Price Ladder"
**Hook:** "Pixels cost $0.10 today. Tomorrow they're $0.50. Watch this."
**Visual:** Screen recording of the live grid + a counter showing pixels sold, with the next-batch price ticking up.
**Body:**
> "Here's how Million Pixels works. The first 100,000 pixels cost 10 cents each. Once those sell out, the next batch jumps to 50 cents. Then a dollar. Then five.
>
> Right now we're 73% through batch one. By tomorrow this batch will be gone.
>
> The earlier you get in, the cheaper your spot on the internet forever."
**CTA:** "Claim a pixel before prices jump → [link]"
**Length:** 25-35 seconds (TikTok), 15s cut for Reels/YouTube Shorts
**Platforms:** TikTok, IG Reels, YouTube Shorts

### Concept 1B: "500 Left"
**Hook:** "Only 487 pixels left at this price. Then we double it."
**Visual:** Close-up of the counter ticking down in real-time. Phone POV.
**Body:** Quick founder cam: "I'm not joking — once batch 1 sells out tonight, the price doubles. Forever."
**Platforms:** TikTok, Reddit (image post + caption)

### Concept 1C: "The Bezos Bet"
**Hook:** "In 2005 someone bought pixels for $1 each. Today they're worth $10K+. We're doing it again."
**Visual:** Side-by-side: 2005 Million Dollar Homepage screenshot → 2026 Million Pixels grid.
**Body:** Cite the history, show the math, end on FOMO.
**Platforms:** TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Twitter video

## ANGLE 2: 👥 SOCIAL PROOF ("Everyone's Doing It")

### Concept 2A: "100K Buyers Wall"
**Hook:** "100,000 people have already bought a pixel. Here's why."
**Visual:** Zoom out from a packed section of the grid showing logos, art, links. Show real names/handles flying past.
**Body:** "[Influencer name] bought one. [Brand name] bought one. This is the new digital real estate."
**Platforms:** TikTok, Twitter, IG

### Concept 2B: "POV: Showing Friends"
**Hook:** "POV: I tell my friends I'm investing in pixels."
**Visual:** Skit-style. Friend reacts skeptical → cut to pixel grid → friend reacts shocked → "I'm in."
**Body:** Relatable, meme-format. ~15 sec.
**Platforms:** TikTok, Reels

### Concept 2C: "Founder Reaction"
**Hook:** "Watching strangers buy my pixels in real-time is wild."
**Visual:** Founder reacts to live dashboard showing purchases ticking in. Bonus: real emotion (tears, laughter).
**Platforms:** TikTok, Twitter

## ANGLE 3: 🤣 HUMOR / MEME ("Pixel Jokes")

### Concept 3A: "My Boomer Dad"
**Hook:** "Trying to explain Million Pixels to my dad."
**Visual:** Split-screen comedy. Dad: "You're selling WHAT?" Son: "Pixels." Dad: 😐 Son: "I made $50K last week." Dad: 🤑
**Platforms:** TikTok, Reels, Twitter video

### Concept 3B: "Pixel Slander"
**Hook:** Stitch a "this is dumb, pixels aren't real" comment from another video, reaction shot: "Bro, you're sending me money for pixels rendered on your phone screen RIGHT NOW."
**Platforms:** TikTok (stitch format), Twitter

### Concept 3C: "Investment Strategy"
**Hook:** "My 2026 investment portfolio: 60% index funds, 30% cash, 10% Million Pixels"
**Visual:** Pie chart meme. Self-aware humor.
**Platforms:** Twitter (image), Reddit, IG

## ANGLE 4: 📖 FOUNDER STORY ("Digital Gold Rush")

### Concept 4A: "Day X of $1M Challenge"
**Hook:** "Day 23 of trying to make $1M in 90 days selling pixels. Here's what happened."
**Visual:** Vlog style. Show daily revenue dashboard. Real numbers, raw transparency.
**Body:** "Today we made $X. We're [X]% to the goal. Here's what's working and what's not."
**Series:** Run as daily/weekly content. Builds parasocial trust.
**Platforms:** TikTok, YouTube, Twitter threads

### Concept 4B: "Why I Quit My Job"
**Hook:** "I quit my $200K job to sell pixels. Here's why."
**Visual:** Founder cam, story arc, end on call-to-buy.
**Platforms:** TikTok, YouTube Shorts

### Concept 4C: "The 2005 Lore"
**Hook:** "In 2005, a 21-year-old made $1M selling pixels. Then he disappeared. We're bringing it back."
**Visual:** Documentary-style. B-roll of old internet, news clippings, then transition to our grid.
**Platforms:** YouTube, TikTok (cut into 60s), Reddit (long-form post)

## ANGLE 5: 🎨 PRODUCT-FORWARD ("Watch It Fill")

### Concept 5A: "Live Grid Timelapse"
**Hook:** "Watch 30,000 pixels get bought in 30 seconds."
**Visual:** Time-lapse of the grid filling. Hypnotic.
**Body:** Minimal voiceover. Let the visual sell it.
**Platforms:** TikTok, IG Reels, Twitter, YouTube Shorts

### Concept 5B: "Pixel Art Reveal"
**Hook:** "Customer paid $500 for this. Watch what they made."
**Visual:** Customer's pixel art being added to the grid, with their story/brand.
**Platforms:** TikTok, IG

### Concept 5C: "Empty to Full"
**Hook:** "We started with 1,000,000 empty pixels. We're at 73% in 45 days."
**Visual:** Day 1 grid → Day 45 grid montage.
**Platforms:** Twitter, LinkedIn, Reddit

## CREATIVE PRODUCTION SCHEDULE

**Phase 1 (Days 1-30):** 3 new organic videos/day × 30 = 90 videos. Top 10 performers become paid creative.

**Phase 2 (Days 31-60):** 5 new paid creative variants/week. Test, kill, scale.

**Phase 3 (Days 61-90):** 10 new paid creative variants/week (creative fatigue at scale).

**Total paid creative needed by day 90:** ~70 variants.

**Production cost target:** $0 in Phase 1-2 (UGC + founder content), $2K-5K in Phase 3 (hire 2-3 UGC creators).
