## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Mission:** Drive $1M revenue in 90 days via organic-first → paid-scale strategy.
**Core Bet:** Validate product-market fit with zero spend (Phase 1), then pour fuel on the fire with 10:1 ROAS paid campaigns (Phase 2-3).
**Key Metric:** 10:1 ROAS minimum on all paid spend ($1 in → $10 out).

## ECONOMIC MODEL

**Average Order Value (AOV) target:** $25 (250 pixels @ $0.10 in early batches; $5-10 once prices rise)
**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) cap:** $2.50 (10:1 ROAS minimum)
**Lifetime Value (LTV) estimate:** $40 (pixel buyers tend to add more as prices rise — FOMO loop)

**Spend math (Phase 2):**
- $350/day × 30 days = $10,500 ad spend
- 10:1 ROAS → $105K revenue from paid
- Plus organic compounding → $300K cumulative by day 60

**Spend math (Phase 3 — scale):**
- $2,000/day × 30 days = $60K ad spend
- 10:1 ROAS → $600K revenue from paid
- Plus organic + word-of-mouth → $1M cumulative by day 90
