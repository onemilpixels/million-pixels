# Million Pixels — Acceptable Use Policy

**Last Updated:** May 13, 2026  
**Effective Date:** Launch Day

---

## Overview

Million Pixels is a digital real estate marketplace where users purchase individual pixels on a shared 1,000,000-pixel canvas. This Acceptable Use Policy (AUP) establishes content standards to keep the canvas family-friendly, legally safe, and focused on legitimate expression.

**By purchasing pixels, you agree to comply with this policy. Violation may result in pixel removal, account suspension, or refund denial.**

---

## ✅ Allowed Content

- Personal art, photography, and creative projects
- Business logos and brand identity (your own business only)
- Charitable messages and cause awareness
- Memes, humor, and internet culture
- Pixel art and retro designs
- Text messages, slogans, and personal expression
- Educational or informational content
- Community messages and shoutouts
- Creative tributes and dedications

---

## ❌ Prohibited Content

### 1. **Explicit / NSFW Content**
No sexually explicit images, nudity, or adult content of any kind.
- Includes: Pornography, erotic imagery, sexually suggestive material
- **Penalty:** Immediate removal + account review

### 2. **Links & URLs**
No clickable links, QR codes, shortened URLs, or embedded hyperlinks.
- **Why:** Prevents spam, malware distribution, and tracking
- **Exception:** Your domain name as plain text (e.g., "example.com") is allowed if it's part of your branding
- **Penalty:** Pixel removal, no refund

### 3. **Hate Speech & Slurs**
No slurs, ethnic slurs, racial epithets, homophobic language, or content promoting discrimination.
- Includes: Hateful messages targeting protected groups
- **Penalty:** Immediate removal + potential account suspension + referral to law enforcement if applicable

### 4. **Cryptocurrency & Scam Content**
No cryptocurrency schemes, pump-and-dump promotions, MLM pitches, or financial scams.
- Includes: Fake investment opportunities, Ponzi schemes, "get rich quick" spam, wallet addresses
- **Penalty:** Account termination + potential law enforcement referral

### 5. **Political Content**
No partisan political campaigning, election interference, or political candidate promotion.
- **Allowed:** General civic messages (e.g., "Vote!" or "Civic Engagement Matters") without partisan affiliation
- **Not allowed:** Candidate names, party logos, campaign slogans, or endorsements
- **Penalty:** Pixel removal, no refund

---

## Gray Areas (Reviewed Case-by-Case)

The following may be flagged for human review. We reserve the right to remove them:
- Violent or gore imagery (even artistic)
- Copyright-infringing content (fan art of major franchises)
- Impersonation of real people or public figures
- Misleading or false information presented as fact
- Spam or repetitive messaging
- Commercial advertising (unless it's your own brand/business)

---

## Enforcement

### Review Process
1. Content is flagged by automated detection or user reports
2. Our team reviews within 24 hours
3. If violation is confirmed, content is removed
4. Account holder is notified with reason for removal
5. Repeat offenders may face account suspension or termination

### Appeals
If you believe your content was removed in error, email **support@millionpixels.com** with:
- Your pixel coordinates
- Description of the content
- Reason you believe it complies with the AUP

We'll respond within 48 hours.

---

## Refund Policy

**Pixels removed due to AUP violations are non-refundable.**

If content is removed for policy violation, you forfeit payment. We reserve the right to donate forfeited funds to charity in your name.

Pixels removed due to technical error (system failure, accidental deletion) *are* eligible for refund.

---

## Prohibited User Behavior

Beyond content, the following account behaviors are prohibited:

- **Spam:** Automated scripts, bulk purchasing for bulk removal, or coordinated campaigns
- **Harassment:** Targeted harassment of other users or staff
- **Payment Fraud:** Chargebacks, stolen payment methods, or fraudulent transactions
- **Manipulation:** Attempting to exploit glitches, duplicate transactions, or circumvent pricing tiers
- **Impersonation:** Pretending to be Million Pixels staff or using official branding deceptively

**Penalty for these behaviors:** Account termination + potential legal action

## What We Monitor For

We use a combination of:
- Automated content filtering (image recognition, keyword detection)
- User reports and community flagging
- Manual review by our team
- Third-party trust & safety services

**We do not sell or share user data with moderation partners.** All reviews are confidential.
